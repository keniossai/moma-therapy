About Jasmine

Lagos

Dr. Boluwatife Rewane is based in lagos and she received her Doctorate in Medical Physiologist from Hospital USA in 2003. Following her professional qualification she practiced Physiotherapy sessions at Hospital and the Royal Hospital. Dr. Boluwatife gained membership of the College of Physiotherapist of USA in 2007. She has over 10 years of experience working general therapy practitioner in both USA and Nigeria
